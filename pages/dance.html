Department of Dance

Welcome to BYU Idaho Dance!

The BYU Idaho Department of Theatre and Dance offers a Bachelor of Arts degree in Dance. This degree is designed to prepare students to find successful career opportunities as a dance professional. During their studies students will acquire the knowledge and experience necessary to become a highly qualified and sound dance educator. The Department of Theatre and Dance also offers a minor in dance.

This site is designed to provide you with contact and program information that will help you to be better informed and complete a program of study more smoothly. Use the navigation menu to the left to locate information that you need.

For information on how to become a dance major check out  "Degrees".

For information on who can apply for scholarships and scholarship deadlines check out "Scholarships".

For information about our Dance Companies including Dance Alliance and Collegiate Dancers click on "Get Involved".

For our dress code click on "What to wear!".

If you have any questions you can email us at dance_dept@byui.edu
