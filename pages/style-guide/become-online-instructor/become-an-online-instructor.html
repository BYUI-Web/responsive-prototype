BYU-IDAHO ONLINE TEACHING

Built Around You

Teaching online is ideal for those who value a flexible work/life schedule. Online instructors are expected to spend 3.5 hours per credit per week in their course (about 10 hours per week for a standard 3-credit course), however, instructors have the flexibility to schedule those hours around their other obligations.

Mission

As an institution of higher learning for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, our instructors have the unique opportunity and obligation to share testimony and gospel perspective while simultaneously teaching respective discipline. By maintaining a wholesome learning environment, students and instructors grow spiritually while receiving and providing a quality temporal education.

Giving back

Thousands of individuals have been blessed as a result of attending a Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints sponsored school. Many who teach at BYU-Idaho do so out of a desire to give back and bless the lives of future generations.
