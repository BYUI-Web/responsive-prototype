The university's Marketing and Communication Team (MarComm), under the direction of PEG and led by University Relations, was established to provide departments with guidance to meet their communication needs.

The primary role of the MarComm Team is to help departments:

  1. Increase consistency of message and brand implementation
  2. Improve quality of messages (with a focus on online communications)
  3. Find efficiencies through increased collaboration with marketing and communication groups currently serving individual departments and offices

Departments operate on a DIY model but come together through MarComm to get strategic guidance, resource suggestions, approval on institutional and off-campus campaigns, and to collaborate with other campus marketers.
