Deferring Financial Aid

Students who intend on deferring a semester(s) for LDS Mission, Medical/Health, or Continuing Education Student reasons, need to consider the following regarding financial aid awards:

FULL POLICY >>
Federal Aid Priority Deadline

This deadline applies to all FAFSA applicants who want their Federal Aid awards released by the tuition payment deadline (typically 1 week after the start of term). Student's must complete and submit all applications, documents, and forms required to process their FAFSA. This includes any verification documents. All requirements need to be completed by the student by this date.

FULL POLICY >>
Financial Aid Determination Date

Schools who disburse Federal Aid are required to evaluate students once a semester to confirm that their federal aid was disbursed correctly. This date is referred to as the Financial Aid Determination Date (FADD).

FULL POLICY >>
Petitions

Petitions and requests for the the adjustment of financial aid awards need to be conducted under the recommendation and consultation of a student's Financial Aid Advisor. Students may contact their Financial Aid Advisor with any questions or concerns regarding the procedures of these requests.
